My Background

My Makeup Look

My Makeup Look

Born and raised in Japan, Ryoko grew up learning to appreciate bringing out their own beauty with effortless makeup technique. Over the 10 years, she has energetically worked as freelance artist for fashion and beauty field that her work is published on Vogue Italy, En vie magazine, Fashion grange and so on. Now she is working with young artists both UK and JPN to tell the world the trend blended original makeup.
